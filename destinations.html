Accursed Mountains Alpine Trek

3 Days hiking in Theth & Valbona.

Day 1 Arrival & Theth Village

Arrive in Shkodër and transfer to the remote village of Theth. Settle into your guesthouse, take a gentle walk to the famous Theth waterfall, and enjoy a traditional Albanian dinner with your host family.

Day 2 Theth to Valbona Pass

The highlight of the trek — a full-day hike crossing the Valbona Pass at 1,800m. Marvel at panoramic views of jagged peaks, descend into the lush Valbona valley, and rest overnight in a riverside guesthouse.

Day 3 Lake Koman Ferry & Departure

A leisurely morning in Valbona before a scenic return via the legendary Lake Koman ferry — one of the most breathtaking boat rides in Europe. Transfer back to Shkodër for departure.

Riviera Coastal Escape

5 Days in Ksamil, Gjipe & Palase.

Day 1 Arrival in Sarandë

Arrive in the lively port town of Sarandë. Take an evening stroll along the promenade, enjoy fresh grilled fish at a harbour-side restaurant, and soak in your first Ionian sunset.

Day 2 Ksamil Island Hopping

Board a small boat and hop between Ksamil's four tiny islands. Snorkel in crystal-clear turquoise water, relax on white-sand beaches, and enjoy a seafood lunch served straight from the boat.

Day 3 Butrint & Palase

Morning visit to the ancient UNESCO city of Butrint — Greek, Roman, and Byzantine ruins set in a stunning lagoon. Afternoon at the dramatic pebble beach of Palase beneath towering cliffs.

Day 4 Gjipe Canyon Hike

Trek through the wild Gjipe canyon, one of Albania's most spectacular hidden gems. Emerge onto a secluded beach accessible only by foot or boat. Lunch at a family-run taverna in Himarë.

Day 5 Leisure & Departure

Free morning to explore Himarë's old quarter or swim one last time. Transfer north to Vlorë for your departure, with stunning riviera views all along the way.

UNESCO Heritage Explorer

2 Days in Berat & Gjirokastër.

Day 1 Berat — City of a Thousand Windows

Explore the iconic Mangalem quarter with its Ottoman white-washed houses stacked up the hillside. Visit the Onufri Icon Museum inside the castle walls, then wind down with a traditional lunch overlooking the Osum river.

Day 2 Gjirokastër — The Stone City

Drive south to Gjirokastër, birthplace of Enver Hoxha and Ismail Kadare. Walk the historic bazaar, tour the imposing Ottoman castle, and descend into the eerie Cold War tunnel hidden beneath the city. Farewell dinner before departure.

Tirana & Lake Bovilla Explorer

3 Days in Tirana & the Albanian Alps foothills.

Day 1 Tirana City Tour & Air Albania Stadium

Start at Skanderbeg Square and explore the colourful streets of the capital. Visit the National History Museum and walk through the lively Blloku district. In the afternoon, head to the iconic Air Albania Stadium — home of the Albanian national football team — for a guided tour of the pitch, dressing rooms, and VIP areas. End the day with dinner at one of Tirana's rooftop restaurants.

Day 2 Lake Bovilla & Bovilla Nature Reserve

Drive 30 minutes from Tirana into the Rrogozhina mountains to reach the stunning turquoise waters of Lake Bovilla. Hike the trails along the reservoir, swim in crystal-clear water, and have a picnic surrounded by pine forests and dramatic peaks.

Day 3 Kruja Castle & Bazaar

Day trip to Kruja, the historic fortress town of national hero Gjergj Kastrioti Skanderbeg. Explore the medieval castle, the Skanderbeg Museum, and the old bazaar packed with traditional crafts and souvenirs before returning to Tirana.
